The Firm

Atelier.AW is a multi-disciplinary architecture and interior design firm based in Bangalore, established in 2026. We create a versatile body of work ranging from bespoke residences to corporate offices and hospitality spaces.

Our distinct style of tropical residential architecture uses local materials and concepts but reinterprets them with a unique and contemporary design sensibility. We believe in designing spaces that are not only visually stunning but also deeply connected to their environment.

Driven by a passion for detail, our studio operates as an open laboratory where ideas are exchanged freely, resulting in projects that are culturally rooted yet globally relevant.

Design Philosophy

Our approach is grounded in three core principles that guide every line we draw and every material we choose.

Contextualism

We believe that architecture should be rooted in one’s environment. Our design is experiential, modulating space to create environments that respond to the site's unique climate and topography.

Holistic Design

We blur the boundaries between architecture, interior design, landscape, and lighting. Every detail is crafted with purpose, ensuring a cohesive narrative throughout the built form.

Sustainability

Sustainability is not an afterthought but a fundamental layer of our process. We prioritize local materials, passive cooling techniques, and energy-efficient systems.

Timelessness

We strive for an aesthetic that transcends trends. By focusing on proportion, light, and honest materiality, we create spaces that age gracefully.
