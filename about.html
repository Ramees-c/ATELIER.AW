About Us

We are a team of architects and designers creating spaces that inspire, last, and connect with their surroundings. Our work is shaped by context, careful detail, and respect for nature.

Architects of Context & Community

Established in 2013 in Kannur, ATELIER.AW is the realization of a shared vision between university cohorts and Principal Architects, Wakif MK and Abdul Kader. What began as an academic alliance has evolved into a premier design studio dedicated to shaping spaces that truly resonate with their environment.

Our Design Philosophy

We believe architecture is a dialogue between the past and the future. Our practice is defined by a seamless fusion of Regional sensibilities and Contemporary aesthetics. We are deeply committed to rooted architecture—prioritizing locally sourced materials to create structures that are not only visually striking but ecologically responsible and climatically appropriate.

A Spectrum of Expertise

Our portfolio is as diverse as the landscapes we build on. Over the last decade, we have successfully delivered projects ranging from:

  • Bespoke Residences & Villas
  • Commercial Complexes & Hospitality Projects
  • Interior Design & Spatial Planning
  • Large-Scale Public Infrastructure
  • Government Projects

With a leadership background that includes rigorous experience with various regulatory frameworks, we bring a high level of professional compliance and technical precision to every commission.

Flexible Engagement: Design & Build

At ATELIER.AW, we adapt to the specific needs of our clients. We offer two distinct modes of engagement:

a. Architectural & Interior Consultancy

For clients who require a visionary design blueprint and technical drawings to guide their own construction teams.

b. Comprehensive Design-to-Key Handover

A stress-free, turnkey solution where we manage the entire lifecycle—from the initial sketch and material selection to construction and final delivery.

Whether creating a single room’s interior or a large-scale public structure, we don't just design spaces; we deliver completed dreams.
